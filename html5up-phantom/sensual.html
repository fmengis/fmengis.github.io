Sensual Co

The Sensual Co is an online retail clothing store that creates and customizes the latest fashion trends. I spoke with the owner and was able to create his website theme on the imagery of his ideas and helped shift the visual aspects to a desirable web page that is interactive and easy to navigate.

The color pallets were carefully chosen to help create theme brand of this companies page and for users to easily read the typography and webpage interaction.
